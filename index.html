Our Products

Low Lint Wipers

Low-lint wipers are specially designed wipes that produce minimal to no lint when used for cleaning or wiping purposes. They are commonly used in sensitive environments such as cleanrooms, laboratories, and electronics manufacturing facilities where even a small amount of lint or debris can cause contamination or damage to the equipment or products being manufactured. The low-lint wipers are made from materials that have been tightly woven or knit to prevent shedding and are often treated with antistatic or other specialized coatings to further minimize the risk of contamination.
